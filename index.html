Company Overview

SaraHydro Engineering Sdn Bhd is a Sarawak-based engineering firm specialising in Mechanical & Electrical (M&E) solutions, infrastructure development, and water system projects. The company delivers end-to-end services including installation, testing, and commissioning.

Mission: To deliver efficient, reliable, and high-quality engineering solutions that create long-term value.

Vision: To become a leading one-stop provider for all mechanical and electrical engineering needs.
