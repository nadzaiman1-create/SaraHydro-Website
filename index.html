About Sara Hydro

Sara Hydro Engineering Sdn Bhd (1359035-M), established in 2020, is an engineering firm specialising in Mechanical & Electrical (M&E) contracting and project management services. The company provides supply, installation, testing, and commissioning services across infrastructure, water systems, and construction projects throughout Sarawak.

Sara Hydro focuses on delivering efficient, reliable, and high-quality engineering solutions that support project performance and long-term value creation.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To be the industry leader in mechanical & electrical field of works by providing the best service to clients through experienced analytic engineering solutions.

Vision: To provide solutions for clients where all mechanical & electrical related works are assured and guaranteed under one roof.
